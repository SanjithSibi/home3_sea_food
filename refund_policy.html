Return Policy

AT MARAKKAAR BIRYANI, WE STRIVE TO DELIVER THE HIGHEST QUALITY BIRYANI AND OTHER FOOD PRODUCTS TO OUR CUSTOMERS. OUR RETURN AND REFUND POLICY IS DESIGNED TO ENSURE YOUR SATISFACTION AND ADDRESS ANY CONCERNS YOU MAY HAVE WITH OUR PRODUCTS. PLEASE READ THE POLICY CAREFULLY BEFORE PLACING YOUR ORDER.

Eligibility for Returns and Refunds
To be eligible for a return or refund, your order must meet the following conditions:
1.The issue must be reported within 24 hours of delivery.
2.The product must be in its original packaging and not consumed more than 20%.
3.A valid proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation) must be provided.

Non-Refundable Situations
We cannot offer refunds or exchanges in the following situations:
1.Any item not in its original condition, damaged, or missing parts for reasons not due to our error.
2.Any item that is returned more than 24 hours after delivery.
3.Personal taste preferences, unless the product is found to be defective or not as described.

Return and Refund Process
Step 1: Contact Us
Email our customer service at support@marakarbiryani.com or call us at +123-456-7890 within 24 hours of receiving your order. Provide your order number, details of the issue, and any relevant photos.

Step 2: Evaluation
Our customer service team will evaluate your request and may contact you for further information. If your request is approved, we will guide you through the return process.

Step 3: Return
If a return is deemed necessary, we will arrange for the product to be picked up from your location at no extra cost to you.

Step 4: Refund
Upon receiving the returned product and inspecting it, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 5-7 business days.

Exchanges
If you received a defective or incorrect item, we are happy to replace it at no additional cost. Please contact us as soon as possible so we can arrange for the exchange.

Late or Missing Refunds
If you haven’t received a refund within the specified time frame, first check your bank account again. Then contact your credit card company, as it may take some time before your refund is officially posted.

If you’ve done all of this and still have not received your refund, please contact us at hi@marakkaar.com.
