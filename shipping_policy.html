Shipping Policy

Delivery Times
We strive to deliver your biryani fresh and on time. Our typical delivery times are:
Monday to Friday: 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM
Saturday and Sunday: 10:00 AM - 11:00 PM

Order Processing Time
Orders placed before 10:00 AM will be processed and delivered the same day.
Orders placed after 10:00 AM may be delivered the next day, depending on availability.

Shipping Fees
Standard Delivery: $5.00
Express Delivery: $10.00 (within 2 hours)
Free delivery for orders over $50.00

Order Tracking
Once your order is confirmed, you will receive an email with the order details and tracking information. You can track your order status on our website under the "Order Tracking" section.

Delivery Instructions
Please provide any specific delivery instructions at the time of checkout. Our delivery team will do their best to accommodate your requests.

Delivery Confirmation
Upon delivery, our team will require a signature or a confirmation code provided at the time of order. This ensures that your biryani is delivered to you safely and correctly.

Handling and Safety
Marakkaar Biryani follows strict hygiene and safety protocols to ensure that your food is delivered in perfect condition. Our delivery personnel are trained to handle food with care, ensuring freshness and quality.

Delivery Issues
If there are any issues with your delivery, such as delays or incorrect items, please contact our customer service team immediately at [Customer Service Email] or [Customer Service Phone Number]. We will resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

Returns and Refunds
Due to the perishable nature of our products, we do not accept returns. If there is a problem with your order, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery for a resolution. We may offer a replacement or a refund depending on the issue.
